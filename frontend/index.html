Features

Service Catalog

Single source of truth for every service, API, and resource. Track ownership, lifecycle, and dependencies in one place.

Health Monitoring

Real-time health checks across all your services. Instant status updates with smart alerting when things go wrong.

AI Auto-Discovery

Automatically discover services from Kubernetes clusters and cloud providers. No manual registration or YAML wrangling.

API Documentation

Aggregate OpenAPI specs, GraphQL schemas, and gRPC definitions automatically. Always up-to-date, never stale.

Dependency Maps

Visualize service relationships and blast radius. Understand exactly what breaks before you deploy a single line of code.

Scorecards

Production readiness checks built in. Track documentation coverage, test quality, and security compliance across all services.

How It Works

Up and running in three steps.

No Helm charts to wrestle. No plugins to configure. No consultants required.

01

Connect

Point Dev Portal at your Kubernetes cluster or cloud account. Our AI scans your infrastructure and discovers services automatically — no agents, no sidecars.

02

Enrich

AI generates missing documentation, identifies ownership gaps, and maps service dependencies. Your catalog goes from zero to complete without anyone writing a YAML file.

03

Operate

Your entire team gets a single pane of glass for every service. Search, monitor, and manage — all from one place. Natural language search included.
